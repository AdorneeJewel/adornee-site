India's Jewellery Legacy

India's jewellery traditions span millennia and geographies. Each region has perfected techniques passed down through generations — from the gemstone-setting mastery of Rajasthan to the intricate filigree work of Bengal. At Adornée, we celebrate this diversity through modern artificial jewellery craftsmanship, honouring tradition while embracing contemporary design for collectors who value cultural artistry.

Delivery

Shipping Information

Global Reach

We ship to 180+ countries worldwide. Adornée jewellery reaches collectors across Europe, Asia, North America, and beyond with secure, insured delivery.

Processing Time

All orders are carefully prepared and quality-checked before dispatch. Typical processing: 3–5 business days from order confirmation.

Tracking

Every order includes tracking details sent to your email. Monitor your shipment from our warehouse to your doorstep in real-time.

Insurance

All shipments are fully insured. In the rare event of damage or loss, we replace your piece immediately.

Delivery Timeframes

  • India: 5–7 business days
  • Asia & Middle East: 10–14 business days
  • Europe & USA: 12–18 business days
  • Other regions: 14–21 business days

* Excludes holidays and weekends

Peace of Mind

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We stand behind our craftsmanship. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked.

What's Eligible

  • ✓ Unworn jewellery in original condition
  • ✓ Original packaging and documentation
  • ✓ Within 30 days of purchase
  • ✓ No damage or alterations

How to Return

  • 1. Contact us with order details
  • 2. Receive prepaid shipping label
  • 3. Package securely and ship back
  • 4. Full refund within 7 days of receipt

Quality Assurance & Exchanges

Received a piece with an issue? We replace it immediately at no cost. Our master artisans ensure every piece meets our rigorous standards. If yours doesn't, we make it right.

Transparency

Privacy Policy

Data Protection

At Adornée, your privacy is paramount. We collect minimal personal information — email, name, and shipping address — solely to process orders and communicate with you. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Secure Payments

All transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL security. We do not store credit card information. Payment processing is handled by certified payment gateways compliant with PCI-DSS standards.

Customer Information

We retain customer information for:

  • • Order fulfillment and tracking
  • • Communication about your purchases
  • • Service improvements and support
  • • Fraud prevention and security

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting hello@adornee.com.

Questions?

For privacy inquiries or data requests, contact privacy@adornee.com
